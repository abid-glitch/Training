Who I Am

Codingal aims to ignite passion in school students to love coding. It enhances problem-solving and logical reasoning abilities. Coding professions represent the future and make up more than 60% of careers in STEM fields.


Coding is a vital skill in the modern world. It enhances how children engage with technology. Research also supports its role in holistic growth, benefiting mental and logical development along with emotional intelligence.
