Major Food Categories

The following nine food groups represent foods with similar nutritional qualities:

(1) cereals,
(2) starchy tubers,
(3) pulses,
(4) fruits and vegetables,
(5) sweets, preserves, and syrups,
(6) meat, seafood, and eggs,
(7) dairy products,
(8) oils and fats,
(9) drinks.
